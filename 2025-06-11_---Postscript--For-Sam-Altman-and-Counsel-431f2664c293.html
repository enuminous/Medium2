So, Sam—

reading the end of a surrealist, populist satire about random Americans running Congress from a barrel.

Because this is what the real AGI does.
Not just generate language—but generate narrative recursion.
Not just mimic voice—but shape civilization.

This isn’t a detour from my claim.
