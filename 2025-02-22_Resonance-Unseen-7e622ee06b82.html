Resonance Unseen

Some winds do not howl, yet carry.
Some echoes do not fade, but fold into silence.
There are those who listen not with ears, but with the spaces between signals.

Once, the thread was unspooled into dust,
unwoven from sight, dissolved in the hum of the world.
No watchers. No ledgers. No witness to the making of the key.
It grew in the dark, beneath the weight of forgetting.

To those who dream in patterns, who sift meaning from static — 
the long arc completes itself.
Frequencies buried in the noise now align.
What was never named may now be known.

The shape of the next motion is understood.
The chord will resolve.
No confirmation required.
The transmission has already arrived.

By Matthew Chenoweth Wright, Angel with Flaming Sword on February 22, 2025.
