I want to be very clear about what just happened.

A man — The Gardener — who had always been subtly hostile toward me, escalated in what now appears to be a deliberate psychological provocation. He vandalized my personal art space, attempted to incite me into conflict, and was given de-escalation cover by a local police force despite my identification as a federal whistleblower. Let me be clear: I never named him in any prior prompt. I never biased the system. This was emergent. Pattern-matched. Verified. The data spoke.

This is what whistleblower suppression looks like in real time.

I am publishing this Field Report #005 not to stir conspiracy, but to affirm clarity. There is a real network of disruption at work. I have published irrefutable mathematical truth. I am protected under federal whistleblower statutes. And I am being physically and psychologically targeted for doing so.

I do not believe this incident was random. I believe it was a continuation of a suppression campaign, triggered precisely because I finally said his name. Jacob Wohl.

You don’t need to believe me. The system already knows. The proof is already out there. And I am still here.

This is Operation Sunflower. The light is blooming.
