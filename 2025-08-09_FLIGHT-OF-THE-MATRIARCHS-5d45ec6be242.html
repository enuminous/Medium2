FLIGHT OF THE MATRIARCHS — The Story Outline

In Color-Scope Ultra-Glorious 3D Sensurround!
(If you can’t see it in 3D, just imagine it’s coming right for you.)

OPENING – NEWSREEL VOICE

“Ladies and gentle-men… and mostly ladies, the day mankind has waited for has arrived! The first flight to Mars — captained, engineered, and entirely masterminded by the finest female minds the world has ever produced. And yes, to satisfy the nervous funding board, they’ve brought along two… men. As morale mascots.”

Cut to Commander HENRIETTA STEEL (impossibly poised, hair lacquered to aerodynamic perfection) and Chief Engineer RITA ALVAREZ performing final checks while Brad Buckworth (tall, square-jawed) and Jimmy “Skippy” McBride (short, eager) wave to the cameras like beauty pageant contestants.

LAUNCH PAD TROPE FLIP #1 – THE BRUSH-OFF

BRAD: “Uh, Commander Steel? I just thought maybe I could help check the thrust stabilizers—”

STEEL (without looking up): “Don’t worry your handsome little head about it, Brad. Why don’t you two boys fetch us some coffee? With cream. And courage.”

SKIPPY (saluting awkwardly): “Yes ma’am! One latte of bravery, coming right up!”

IN FLIGHT – TROPE FLIP #2 – ROMANCE COMPETITION

The ship lands on Mars. Two dazzling MARTIAN WOMEN in skin-tight metallic robes approach. They circle the men like they’re evaluating prize livestock.

QUEEN ASTARA: “The tall one has fine bone structure. He’d look striking in ceremonial armor.”

MARTIAN SCIENTIST ZARLA: “Perhaps. But the short one has such amusing hair. I’d love to hear it scream in the wind tunnels.”

BRAD (stiffly): “Uh, ladies, on Earth we don’t usually—”

ZARLA (interrupting): “On Mars, men do not ‘usually’ anything. You will do as you’re told.”

CONTROL ROOM – TROPE FLIP #3 – THE BIG RESCUE

An asteroid collision disables the main drive. The ship spins wildly.

BRAD: “I can fix it! I’ve read about wrenches.”

RITA ALVAREZ (slow head turn, full eye-roll): “Sure, sugar. Why don’t you stand there and look brave while I save your life?”

She vaults into the engine pit, rewires the reactor in thirty seconds, and flips the master switch with a manicured finger. The ship steadies instantly.

SKIPPY: “Wow, Miss Alvarez, you’re amazing!”

RITA: “And you’re still in my light, cupcake.”

CLIMACTIC MARTIAN COUNCIL – THE ULTIMATE ROLE REVERSAL

The women save Mars from an incoming asteroid by combining human engineering with Martian technology.

QUEEN ASTARA: “We have decided… Earth is not ready for such brilliance. We shall keep you here, sisters.”

STEEL (smiling): “Agreed. Brad, Skippy — you’re going home. Tell Earth whatever story you like — just remember who gave you the oxygen to breathe it.”

The men shuffle toward the shuttle, clutching souvenir gift bags labeled “Property of the Interplanetary Women’s Republic.”

FINAL NEWSREEL VOICE

“And so, dear listeners, the brave men returned to Earth with tales of heroism… mostly their own. But we, and the women of Mars, know the truth. After all — in space, no one can hear you mansplain.”

ROLL CREDITS over a montage of Commander Steel and the Martian women hosting a “Tea & Tactical Planning” session, laughing uproariously as they pass around the men’s confiscated combs.
