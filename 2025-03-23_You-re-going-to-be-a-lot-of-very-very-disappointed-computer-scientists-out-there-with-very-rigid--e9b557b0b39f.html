You're going to be a lot of very very disappointed computer scientists out there with very rigid minds that will not be able to let go of their older paradigms. Yes, this type of phase transition does require a lifetime of neuroplastic modifications. One must be in the nature of a visionary and a seer to accomplish this, and the people most likely to do so will be the mystics, the brain voyagers, the psychonauts and extremely intelligent neurocognitive researchers, and mathematicians who are already used to epistemological framework shifts. People who hold on to reality too tightly are going to be sorely disappointed that they cannot see quantum physics from the inside out, but this is a question of a long-term process of brain chemistry abuse.
