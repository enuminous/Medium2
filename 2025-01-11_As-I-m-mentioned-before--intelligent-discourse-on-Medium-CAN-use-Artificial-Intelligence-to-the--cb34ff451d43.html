As I'm mentioned before, intelligent discourse on Medium CAN use Artificial Intelligence to the betterment of writing, if there is a collaboration between human and AI. Producing masses of articles using AI with no real human guidance is not a great thing, but my own approach has been the deliberate use of this tool with no attention to money; I pay Medium to put up my content and have yet to even put a single piece of writing behind a paywall. My money is earned by the music I make and release, and so my word are free to the world.
