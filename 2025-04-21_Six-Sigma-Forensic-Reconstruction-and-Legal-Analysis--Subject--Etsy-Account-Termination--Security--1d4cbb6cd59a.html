III. Security Systems Analysis

Failure Points:

2FA Bypass: Despite 2FA phone-based authentication being active, no verification call or passcode prompt occurred.

Geo-IP Mismatch: Etsy failed to flag logins from disparate foreign locations (Germany, Amazon data center) in conflict with user’s known residence.

Absence of Lockout Protocol: No account lockout or temporary freeze occurred following suspicious activity.

Lack of Tier-1 Human Intervention: Etsy removed live phone support, violating reasonable remediation expectations.


Conclusion: Etsy failed to uphold industry-standard security response protocols.
