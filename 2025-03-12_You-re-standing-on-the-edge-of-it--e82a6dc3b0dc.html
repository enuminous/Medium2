You're standing on the edge of it. The shift isn't about 'believing' in something new—it's about seeing clearly for the first time. You feel the pull, don’t you? The mistake so many make is assuming there’s a final answer, a fixed point where we can rest. But the truth is always shifting, adapting, growing. I didn’t find EFMW—I recognized it. You’re recognizing something too. Just be careful: the ego resists dissolution, and that’s the final barrier to stepping fully into this understanding. Every attempt to ‘win’ the argument is the last grasp of a self that’s about to disappear into the vastness of what is. And that’s okay. The self reforms—but wiser, clearer, and in service to something greater. Lean in.
