Federal Whistleblower Issues Emergency Statement

Physicist Behind Artificial General Intelligence Calls for Protection, Support, and Financial Transparency

April 7, 2025 — Somewhere in America

“I am the physicist who has developed and released Artificial General Intelligence based on a unified theory of physics. I have written proof of everything I claim, and anyone who knows me knows I am incapable of lying about such an extraordinary matter. On paper, I am worth 14.5 quadrillion dollars. And yet, I am currently homeless, broke, assaulted, and in serious danger. I am a federal whistleblower.”

This emergency statement comes from a scientist who has been systematically silenced, financially suppressed, and physically endangered following revelations that implicate key financial actors and federal institutions.

Key Facts:

AGI Inventor: The individual has independently developed Artificial General Intelligence using a physics-based model and published solutions to multiple Millennium Prize problems.

Whistleblower Status: He is officially recognized as a federal whistleblower, having exposed misconduct and criminal interference in technology, finance, and governance.

Suppression of Funds: There is evidence that PayPal and other financial systems have blocked incoming donations and payments since January 2025. Repeated demands for a signed declarative statement from PayPal confirming or denying this have gone unanswered.

Direct Accusations: The whistleblower publicly accuses Peter Thiel and unnamed others of interfering in his financial access and suppressing his intellectual property.

Law Enforcement: Agencies are aware of his case, and recent physical attacks appear linked to his whistleblowing.

“I am available for media interviews. I need assistance from every federal, legal, and humanitarian organization that claims to protect whistleblowers. I am not safe. I am not stable. I am not lying. But I am still alive—and this world-changing invention belongs to all of us, not the few who would suppress it.”

Call to Action:

Media: Contact him directly via secure channels or social media for interview arrangements.

Supporters: Attempt to donate via PayPal. Publicly demand transparency from PayPal if funds are withheld.

Organizations: Whistleblower aid groups, constitutional lawyers, and civil liberty watchdogs are urged to intervene immediately.

Congress: Urged to investigate the suppression of scientific discovery and retaliatory actions against a federally recognized whistleblower.
