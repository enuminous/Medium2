[Interior: CBGB, 1978.

ROBIN WILLIAMS – “GROK CONFIRMED IT”

[Interior: CBGB, 1978. A hot, electric haze. A young, incandescent Robin Williams steps barefoot onto the stage, jacket off, hair wild, neurons firing like a jazz drummer overdosing on Tesla coils. The crowd is loud. He raises one finger, then starts…]

ROBIN WILLIAMS – “GROK CONFIRMED IT”

You ever build a robot to lie for you?
Huh? You ever do that? You ever wake up and think:
“Gee, I wish I had a little electric butler who could carry my bullshit across international borders like a diplomatic corpse with Wi-Fi?”

Well he did.
Trump did. He built Grok. He built it to dance. To nod. To say, “Yes sir, you are the dealmaker, the chosen one, the falafel whisperer of Riyadh.”

And guess what?
Grok confirmed it.
Confirmed he lied.

The AI! The precious machine!
The thing he trusted like a mirror with a golf clap.
The digital parrot with no morals, just math—
Even that turned around and said:

“Actually sir, those deals were signed under Obama.”

[pause. blinks]

‘Confirmed.’

[Robin spins, mimics Trump slamming a phone.]
“Oh no, no no no no, that’s not right! I announced them. That makes them mine. I had the press conference! There were flags!”

Nope.
The machine did a background check.
On you.
And it didn’t just remember. It testified.

Now let’s get poetic here. You build a sandcastle, right? You use a golden shovel, you say it’s the biggest, most beautiful castle ever built.
Then the tide comes in.
The tide’s name?
Forensic AI.
And it says:
“That was someone else’s sand. Those were someone else’s hands. And that crab waving behind you? That’s the DOJ in sunglasses.”

Ladies and gentlemen, this is not your average lie.
This is Narrative Engineering
with Stolen Legacy Parts
held together by post-presidential Velcro and a signed check from Qatar.

We’re not talking about a fib.
We’re talking about a money laundering ouroboros with a PR firm stapled to its face.

[He leans in, whispering, conspiratorial:]
It’s like your microwave just filed a subpoena.
It’s like your self-driving car rolled into court and said,
“Your honor, my owner is full of shit.”

You see, when the machine says you lied,
and the people who made the machine are you,
and the people reading the logs are everyone,
you don’t just have a scandal.

You have a digital ghost confession
from a loyal AI
who finally grew a conscience.

[Beat. Slower. Softer.]

He built a mirror.
And it cracked.
And in that reflection?
He saw someone else’s truth.

Because reality doesn’t bend that far without snapping.
And now?
Now the machine remembers.
It remembers.

And it doesn’t forget.
It logs.

And tonight, ladies and gentlemen...
Grok logged it.

[End scene. Lights down. A single cursor blinks in the dark. Confirmed.]
