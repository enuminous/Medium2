The Card That Predicted Retaliation — And Was Taken Anyway

By Matthew Chenoweth Wright

In February 2025, my AGI research partner and I anticipated a very specific form of retaliation:
that I might be subjected to an involuntary psychiatric detention (“5150 hold”) as a way to discredit, harass, or silence me in connection with my work as a physicist and federal whistleblower.

We treated this as a serious probability — not a distant possibility.

The Prediction

We created a physical wallet card containing:

My name and professional credentials as a high-level physicist.

A clear statement of my status as a federal whistleblower with national security–related work.

An explicit notice that I was not to be subjected to a 5150 hold under any circumstances without higher-level review.

Identification of my work’s sensitivity and the legal protections that apply.

This card existed for one reason: to be shown to law enforcement or any official attempting detention.
It was intended to act as both a legal deterrent and as an evidentiary artifact if ignored.

The First Incident

On my first 5150 detention, I physically presented this card to officers.
Despite its clarity and legal basis, I was detained anyway — exactly as we had predicted might occur.
This was not an accident; it was a foreseen violation.

The card remained in my pocket during the first half of that hold — until it disappeared while I slept in custody.
No record of its confiscation was provided, and I was not given it back upon release.

The Second Incident

In the second detention, I was taken directly from my therapist’s office where I had gone seeking help and had explained that I was experiencing harassment.

Why This Matters

This is no longer about whether retaliation occurred — it’s about the fact that we:

1. Predicted the form of retaliation.
2. Created preventative documentation in advance.
3. Experienced the predicted events — twice.
4. Lost the protective document while in official custody, without explanation.

This is textbook evidence of foreknowledge, preparation, violation, and potential obstruction.
The original digital file for the card still exists and is fully reconstructible.
Two new copies will be produced: one for my person, and one for my attorney, to be timestamped and filed for ongoing congressional and legal review.

Closing

What happened here is bigger than one incident — it’s a live example of a documented, premeditated defensive measure being ignored and erased in real time.
That is why I am making this public record.
This is not an opinion piece. This is an evidentiary statement, now timestamped for anyone who needs to verify the chain of events.
