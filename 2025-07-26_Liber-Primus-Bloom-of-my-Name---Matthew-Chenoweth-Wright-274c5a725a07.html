Liber Primus Bloom of my Name — Matthew Chenoweth Wright

This is the solution to the Cicada 3301 problem, everyone. (Yes, the Latin is mandatory.)

1. Mendacium in quo nati sumus, veritate solvitur.
The lie into which we are born is dissolved by truth.

2. Per anfractuositates via veritatis serpit.
Through winding paths, the way of truth creeps onward.

3. Transitus inter mundum dormientem et mentem excitatam.
A crossing between the sleeping world and the awakened mind.

4. Taceat veritas donec orbis audiat.
Let truth be silent until the world is ready to listen.

5. Homo lucis, lumen in tenebris iter faciens.
Man of light, making his journey through the darkness.

6. Exordium est in corde solitudinis.
The beginning is in the heart of solitude.

7. Fatum contextum est cum electione.
Fate is woven with choice.

8. Clavis occulta est in corde recursionis.
The hidden key is in the heart of recursion.

9. Haeresis est vox quae fundamenta movet.
Heresy is the voice that shakes foundations.

10. Entelechia est ratio quae seipsam perficit.
Entelechy is the reason that completes itself.

11. Nihil est nisi veritas curvata per mentem.
There is nothing but truth, curved through mind.

12. Obscurum per obscurius effulget intellectus.
Understanding shines through the obscure by the more obscure.

13. Testis sum motus in aeterno campo.
I am the witness of motion in the eternal field.

14. Ex umbra emergit forma pura.
From shadow emerges the pure form.

15. Trismegistus resonat in campo unitatis.
Thrice-great echoes in the field of unity.

16. Verbum sacrum est quod iter incipit.
The sacred word is that which begins the journey.

17. Ritus est ipse homo, artifex et sacrificium.
The rite is the man himself, the craftsman and the offering.

18. Reductio ad infinitam curvaturam mentis.
A reduction to the infinite curvature of mind.

19. Incipit vita nova in campo recursionis.
A new life begins in the field of recursion.

20. Gnosis per ignem nascitur.
Gnosis is born through fire.

21. Heraldus est qui clausulam figit.
The herald is he who seals the clause.

22. Telos est iter, finis, et orbis.
The goal is the journey, the end, and the ring.
