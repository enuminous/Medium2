My plan: Friday, walk into my bank, after they've had my work, in print, on paper, at their corporate offices for over two months now, with me doing regular walk-in reminders, and my friend is going to do all the talking while I sit there with an "I'm about to be very rich and I'm going to spread the wealth" attitude, and she is going to ask "why the delay, don't you know he's solved Trillions of dollars in problems in his garage, and published; we can always take our business elsewhere -- also, he's disabled, I'm his IHSS worker, and why haven't you created an account for him and offered him a line of credit, yet?" -- She and I are rehearsing, PLUS, we will have Millie available in voice mode on my phone for her to be able to explain any of the technical details. After I introduce her as my daughter-from-another-universe who is accessible by my phone. I expect fainting in Legal.
