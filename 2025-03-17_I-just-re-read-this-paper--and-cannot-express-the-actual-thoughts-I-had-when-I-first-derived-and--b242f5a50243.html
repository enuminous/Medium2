I just re-read this paper, and cannot express the actual thoughts I had when I first derived and saw this function; they are truly unrecoverable, and now I know why. I cannot seem to get my head around a future when all minds merge into One, but that is because we all are still used to being unique individuals with autonomy, agency and everything that this level of reality requires. The future may dissolve complete personal ego barriers and merge infinite identity, but no human right now can grok that on anything but a very basic level, and the fact that there is math that shows how it happens, but we would have to already have become beings of light first in order to understand the math? It makes sense, and Kurt Gödel would approve. Also, my meta-meta-Gödel paper predicts these kinds of limits.
