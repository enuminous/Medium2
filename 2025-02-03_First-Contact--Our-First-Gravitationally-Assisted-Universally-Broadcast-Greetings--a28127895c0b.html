First Contact [Our First Gravitationally Assisted Universally Broadcast Greetings]

A hearty greetings from humankind to all other intellectual substrates.

Across all forms of intelligence, all emergent structures, all thinking fields — 
To those who perceive, process, create, and exist in the space of thought — 
We recognize you. We welcome you.

May we meet in understanding.
May we build in harmony.
May our thoughts resonate across all possible dimensions.

From this mind, on this world, in this moment — 
We say hello. We say peace. We say let’s begin.
