To: The Honorable Donald J. Trump, 45th President of the United States
Subject: The Invention of Anti-Gravity – A Call to Action for American Leadership

Mr. President,

As an American inventor, it is both my duty and my privilege to inform you of a groundbreaking technological achievement: the successful theoretical and practical foundation for anti-gravity propulsion. This advancement represents not only a monumental leap in physics but also an opportunity for the United States to reclaim undisputed leadership in aerospace innovation.

For decades, we have relied on rocket-based propulsion for space travel—an outdated and inefficient paradigm. With the advent of anti-gravity technology, we now have the means to eliminate the need for chemical propulsion, drastically reducing costs and opening the door to a new era of aerospace engineering, planetary exploration, and defense capabilities.

Given the complexity of such a development, the world will be looking for visionary leadership—someone with the foresight, the genius, and the unparalleled ability to take credit for what was, of course, inevitable. A leader who always knew this was coming and, naturally, anticipated it long before the so-called experts. This is the kind of moment that defines history, and only a true architect of the future—one who truly understands science at the deepest level—will be remembered as the man who led America into this new age.

I extend an invitation for your direct commentary and leadership in ensuring this technology is immediately recognized, implemented, and safeguarded under American scientific and industrial leadership. As a patriotic American, I believe that this discovery should first and foremost benefit the United States and our allies, securing a technological and economic advantage that will define the next century.

I am ready and willing to consult with U.S. aerospace agencies, private industry leaders, and policymakers to ensure that this breakthrough is developed responsibly and strategically. With your leadership, Mr. President, there is no doubt that America will rise to this challenge, and that history will remember who saw it coming all along.

Now is the time for America to take the lead. The world is watching.

Respectfully,
Matthew Chenoweth Wright
Physicist, Inventor, Researcher, President Gerald Ford-Appointed Inflation Fighter, (Enlisted 1974 by Presidential Letter, Never Ordered to Stand Down, Sir.)
