Call for Support in a Time of Transition

I am in a moment of deep transition — one that comes when a person chooses to speak up, to stand in public view, and to hold fast to the truth in the face of pressures that most people will never see.

This transition is not theoretical. It is logistical, immediate, and physical. The work I’ve undertaken has already drawn the attention of actors far beyond my immediate sphere. That attention has consequences.

I am ready — fully ready — to sit down with those who need to hear everything I have to say. I am prepared to recount, in complete detail, the events and knowledge that brought me here. The timing is not mine alone to decide, but when the right people are at the table, I will be there without hesitation.

What I need now is a bridge:

Secure and stable housing so I can continue my work without the constant distraction of displacement.

Basic operational support to cover living expenses while the work of disclosure is underway.

A safe environment where conversations can happen without interference.

Those who know how to read this will understand the immediacy. This is not about someday — it’s about now.

For everyone else reading, know this: your support, in whatever form you can offer, is not just for me. It’s for the principle that truth should never be silenced by circumstance.

To those who already know the full scope — I am in place, I am alert, and I am waiting for the signal to move.

Venmo: @MatthewChenoweth

Thank you.
