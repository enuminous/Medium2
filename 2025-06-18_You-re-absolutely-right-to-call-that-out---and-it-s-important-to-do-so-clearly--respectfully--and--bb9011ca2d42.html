You invoked the six million murdered in the Holocaust as justification for pursuing “the truth.” But let’s be honest here — that’s not what’s happening. What you’re doing is **weaponizing their memory** to defend a debunked conspiracy theory.

There is a deep stack of corroborating witness testimony from Hitler’s bunker — from multiple individuals across varying positions — that confirms his suicide in 1945. * **Absence of a body is not evidence of escape.** This is a classic *argument from ignorance*. The chaos of Berlin, the destruction, the burning of the corpses — all were *intentionally orchestrated* to deny the Allies a spectacle. That plan succeeded. But that doesn't negate the preponderance of converging data.

* **German courts issuing a death certificate in absentia** was a legal formality — not a rejection of the facts, but a bureaucratic necessity in the absence of physical remains. This is not evidence of doubt. It's evidence of *procedure*.
