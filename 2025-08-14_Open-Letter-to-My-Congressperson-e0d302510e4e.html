I am requesting:

Immediate congressional inquiry into the handling of DOJ case 646675-CSJ.

Whistleblower protections applied to both myself and my work, including the EFMW framework.

Direct logistical and financial support to ensure my safety and ability to continue my research while this matter is resolved.

I believe my situation has now escalated beyond an individual civil rights matter into a question of DOJ integrity and whistleblower safety. I ask for your urgent attention and direct response.

Sincerely,
Matthew Chenoweth Wright
