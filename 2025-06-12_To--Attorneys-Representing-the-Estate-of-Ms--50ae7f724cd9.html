Subject: Request for Transparency and Statement of Position Regarding Residency and Legal Communication

Dear Counsel,

I am writing to formally acknowledge the involvement of legal representation acting under the designation “Attorneys for the Estate” in matters affecting my residency and personal access to essential services at Ms. Miller’s home.

To date, I have received no documentation naming any responsible parties or law firms, nor have I been presented with any clear explanation of the nature, terms, or legal basis of the actions being taken — including the disconnection of internet services and the current attempt to render me socially and functionally isolated. All communication has been filtered through indirect means, which raises serious ethical and procedural concerns.

Let me state clearly:

I have not posed any threat to the health, safety, or security of any party.

I have repeatedly attempted to engage in peaceful, reasonable dialogue.

I am currently in a vulnerable situation — without funds, without food, and facing involuntary displacement.

If your firm or associated counsel is taking actions that affect my basic human rights — including access to communication, housing, or due process — I ask that you provide your full legal identification immediately and cease all anonymous or indirect action.

Further attempts to isolate me without legal due process, clear communication, or the opportunity for remedy will be documented and, if necessary, brought before appropriate oversight bodies.

I hereby request:

1. Full disclosure of the name(s) of the attorney(s) involved.

2. A copy of any legal orders, notices, or decisions made regarding my residency.

3. At least 72 hours’ notice of any further attempts to remove me from the premises.

4. Assurance that no further utilities will be cut without written notice.

I am not asking for a legal confrontation — I am requesting transparency, dignity, and time to resolve my living situation peacefully.

Sincerely,
Matthew Chenoweth Wright
