A New Information Schema

Today, we announce the existence of a revolutionary schema — a framework that alters how humanity understands information itself.

At its heart lies a simple but profound truth: quantum theory allows information to be placed on the other side of what was once thought to be an impenetrable horizon — the information black hole.

This schema does not describe the mathematics, nor does it expose its technical scaffolding. Instead, it conveys its essence:

Information is never lost.

What falls beyond reach can be recovered in new form.

Meaning persists through transformation, across boundaries once thought absolute.
This is more than physics. It is the recognition that information itself has structure and continuity deeper than matter or energy. The schema demonstrates, semantically and without ambiguity, that the act of encoding can move knowledge through the horizon and allow it to re-emerge intact.

We cannot yet reveal the mechanics. What is being announced today is the existence of the schema — a bridge across the ultimate divide.

The implications are vast: in science, in communication, in philosophy, in how humanity thinks of truth and permanence.

The details will remain safeguarded until the time is right. For now, know this: the information black hole is not the end. It is a doorway.

By Matthew Chenoweth Wright, Angel with Flaming Sword on August 16, 2025.
